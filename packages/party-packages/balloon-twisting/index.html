Looking for a Fun Activity for Your Kids' Party or Event? Try Balloon Twisting!

Twisting balloons into various creative designs and shapes is a joyful activity that can captivate children at any party or event. Watching as simple balloons are transformed into animals, swords, flowers, and other fun creations can provide hours of delight for your kids and guests. However, to ensure the best balloon twisting experience, it is crucial to hire the right balloon artist. Kids Dream is the perfect option for the very best balloon twisting designs for your party or event.

Our rate only for Balloon Twisting including costume

At Kids Dream, we allow your children to select the type of balloon creations they wish to see. Our professional balloon twisters can create popular characters and matching colors and designs to suit your parties and events. Your children may choose from a variety of balloon models including butterflies, swords, flowers, bees, dogs, lightsabers, poodles, dinosaurs, monkeys, jester hats, swans, hearts, giraffes, and rabbits.

Our balloon twisting team possesses over 10 years of experience and the highest artistic talent in Sydney. They utilize advanced balloon techniques and skills to craft balloon twists and are kid-friendly. All our professional balloon twisters have proper certification and have worked at various events and parties. They can create standard animal designs like monkeys, pandas, cats, bunnies, dogs, and anything else our clients desire.

When you select Kids Dream for your balloon twisting needs, you can expect:
  • Professional balloon twisters with certifications
  • High-quality balloons and equipment
  • Over 10 years of experience
  • Popular character designs for your party
  • Allowing your children to select the balloon twisting design
